Our Story

More Than a Church — A Family Since 1921

"Everyone is someone, and everyone is someone special."

For over a century, Prince Chapel by the Sea has been a cornerstone of the La Jolla community. Rooted in the African Methodist Episcopal tradition, we are a welcoming congregation dedicated to faith, fellowship, and service to all.


Whether you're seeking a spiritual home, connection with others, or simply a place of peace — our doors are always open to you.
